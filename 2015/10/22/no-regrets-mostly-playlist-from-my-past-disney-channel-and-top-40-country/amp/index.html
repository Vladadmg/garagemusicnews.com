by Maddy Siiter

Most of what I remember from 2007-2011 is shown in this throwback playlist. I was a devoted Disney Channel fan who constantly wore out the batteries of her Hello Kitty portable CD player listening to the High School Musical 2 soundtrack or the newest Jonas Brothers album. When I turned 12, I replaced the portable CD player for an iPod and thought I was “too old” for the Jonas Brothers (are we ever really too old for the JoBros?). I became more of a Top 40 country radio girl and wanted to pack my bags for Nashville, Tennessee. Although it’s nothing like I listen to now, it was fun to reminisce about what I had grown up with. *digs around closet for Hannah Montana CD*

Here’s a list of some of my favorite throwback songs:

  1. “What Time Is It” – High School Musical 2 soundtrack (2007)
  2. “Year 3000” – The Jonas Brothers (2007)
  3. “Potential Breakup Song” – Aly and AJ (2007)
  4. “7 Things” – Miley Cyrus (2008)
  5. “Nobody’s Perfect” – Hannah Montana (2008)
  6. “You Belong With Me” – Taylor Swift (2009)
  7. “The House That Built Me” – Miranda Lambert (2010)
  8. “American Honey” – Lady Antebellum (2010)
  9. “Heaven in the Middle of Nowhere” – Caroline County (2011)
  10. “Crazy Girl” – Eli Young Band (2011)
