We’re looking to add new writers and photographers for Garage Music News. Deadline for applications is 12/23.

In addition to contributing, you’ll be given feedback on your writing and/or photos, as well as offered an internship with THE GARAGE for your resume.

Garage Music News is a way for young writers, photographers, and/or videographers with a variety of experience levels to get involved. GMN is a digital and physical zine based out of THE GARAGE and focused on the music important to the all-ages scene. GMN’s contributors have reviewed local albums, interviewed national artists, talked about controversies, created Best Of lists, and covered shows at the THE GARAGE, Varsity Theater, Triple Rock, Myth, Mill City Nights, and First Ave.

THE GARAGE, Twin Cities Catalyst Music, and GMN’s Managing Editor, Sararosa, are excited for you to join the team. Find all the position and internship descriptions here.
