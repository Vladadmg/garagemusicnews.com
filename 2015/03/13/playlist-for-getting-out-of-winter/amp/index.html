by Cassie

It’s cold. It’s wet. It’s annoying. We’re all ready to be done with this perpetual winter. Thankfully, the weather is finally starting to warm up and it’s time to awake from our winter hibernation. However, this is easier said than done. So I’ve created a playlist to give us all the energy and motivation needed to get ready for spring and the warm weather. Included are songs about my embitterment towards winter, as well as a few songs to get you dancing out in the nice weather.

1. “Sick Of It” by Skillet

This winter seems to be dragging on forever and I, for one, am SICK OF IT!

2. “My Heart Escapes” by Blindside

3. “I’m So Sick” by Flyleaf

I’m so sick of this cold and tiring weather, I’m ready for spring!

4. “Dance (Blow It All Away)” by House Of Heroes

Let’s dance and blow this agonizing winter away, while jamming to some House Of Heroes.

 

5. “Make A Move” by Icon For Hire

No more laying around inside! It’s getting warmer out  and it’s time to make a move.

 

6. “Cray Button” by Family Force 5

Let’s get cray in this warm weather and have some fun!

 

7. “I Feel So Alive” by Capital Kings

The end of winter is so close and I feel so alive just thinking about all the fun this summer will bring.

 

8. “Adrenaline” by Nine Lashes

It can be very hard to find the energy needed to get out of our winter sleep, but with some adrenalinewe can make it through to spring.

9. “Fire It Up” by Thousand Foot Krutch

This song is simply just to fire you up for the end of winter.

 

10. “High Of 75” by Relient K

I have been waiting so long to see a high of 75 and it’s finally getting close to that time of year again.

 

11. “Supernova Sunrise” by We Are Leo

Winter’s almost over and it’s finally getting nice out. Get off your butt and go outside!
