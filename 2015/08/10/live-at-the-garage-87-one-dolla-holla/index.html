LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 8/7: One Dolla Holla

Posted on August 10, 2015 by THE GARAGE in Show Photos // 0 Comments

by Riley

Photos from a packed line-up of the latest installment in the One Dolla Holla series! The show featured: Villain of the Story, Insomniac, ROYAL’Z, Alistair Hennessey, If I Ran The Zoo, RAWHYDE, Molly’s Worst Enemy, Pets With People Names, Failure Meets Fame, Telepathist, nanashi, Knockout Kid, and Main State!
