Want to Contribute?

Garage Music News is a youth-run music magazine that strives to be a voice for the Twin Cities all-ages music scene.

Internship applications are open! We’re looking for the following positions from January-June to join our awesome returning contributors:

–Senior Staff Writer (1 spot)

–Staff Writer (3 spots)

–Staff Photographer (1 spot)

 

Also, if you have a piece you’d like us to take a look at for publishing (like a review, interview, or profile), send it our way: garagemusicnews@gmail.com and copy our advisor at logan@catalystmusic.org

Or, send us a pitch for an article you’d like to write.

Questions:

Email us at garagemusicnews@gmail.com, Subject Line: Question about [topic of your question]
