ANNOUNCEMENT | Write for THE GARAGE

Posted on November 20, 2014 by garage17 Leave a comment

CALL FOR STUDENT WRITERS

Garage Music News is searching for music-loving young people to expand our weekly music coverage this winter and in the spring semester. We want writers in their mid and late teens from all experience levels interested in contributing on their own schedule any of the following:

-Profiles of local and/or national acts

-Reviews of all-ages shows

-Reviews of albums of local and/or national acts

-Interviews of local and/or national acts

-Posts about happenings in the local and/or national music world

In addition to having a large audience, Garage Music News writers will be building a portfolio of writing and also receive editing and feedback from GMN’s editor to ensure their writing is continually improving. This is a great chance to contribute to the Twin Cities music scene.

Our next two open meetings are in St Paul, at our partner MOI-MSP, Mon. Dec. 1 and Mon. Dec. 15. from 6-8pm. If you’re interested and plan on attending email Garage Music News editor, Logan, by Saturday, Nov 29 at garagemusicnews@gmail.com.

MOI-MSP

2388 University Ave W

St Paul MN 55114

(Their storefront is right on the Green Line.)
