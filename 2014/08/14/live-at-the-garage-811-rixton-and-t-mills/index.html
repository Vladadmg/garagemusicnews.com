LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 8/11: Rixton and T. Mills

Posted on August 14, 2014 by garage17 Leave a comment

by Riley

On Monday, August 11, Rixton and T. Mills performed a SOLD OUT show at THE GARAGE, put on by Live Nation! Rixton, fresh off a performance on Late Night with Seth Meyers and the release of their single “Wait On Me,” Garage Music News contributor Riley was there to capture every move and sing along song.
