REVIEW | Tonight Alive’s successful second release “The Other Side”

June 10, 2014 // 1 Comment

If you haven’t yet heard anything from Tonight Alive, it’s likely that you will be seeing more about them within the next few years. Originating from Sydney, Australia, the pop-punk rock band is on the rise within the rock community. Tonight Alive released their first album What Are You So Scared Of? in late 2011. Previously, the band (comprised of guitarists Whakaio Taaki and Jake Hardy, bassist Cam Adler, drummer Matt Best, and vocalist Jenna McDougall) had released three EPs in 2010 and 2011. After two years of touring with other bands around the world and in their home country, Tonight Alive released The Other Side in September of 2013. Following this release was their first headlining tour across the US, which even stopped here in Minnesota at THE GARAGE. [...]
