LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 6/21: THRIVE Fest, photos and gifs

Posted on June 24, 2014 by THE GARAGE in Uncategorized // 0 Comments

by Tanner

THE GARAGE and Zombie Boardshop Presents: souTh of tHe RIVEr skate & music fest! It was a free outdoor event with music, food, a dunk tank, and more with all raffle proceeds donated to charity and the skatepark for renovation!

Bands included Vaudeville, Kick, Words For Penga, Modern Side, Hellen Keller (BAND), Kittyjangles, and The Modern Era on THE GARAGE mainstage with Nani, Buhdihkuh, Grayson Dewolfe, From The Heart, Lindsay Kappa, and Riri Flaherty in the Capitol Guitars Acoustic Lounge.

Thanks to fellow sponsors Zombie Boardshop, Capitol Guitars, and Mad Moose Ink.
