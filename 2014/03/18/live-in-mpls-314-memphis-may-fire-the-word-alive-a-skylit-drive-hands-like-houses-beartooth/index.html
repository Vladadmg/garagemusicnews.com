LIVE IN MPLS | 3/14: Memphis May Fire, The Word Alive, A Skylit Drive, Hands Like Houses & Beartooth

Posted on March 18, 2014 by THE GARAGE in Uncategorized // 1 Comment

[Editor’s note: This post of Memphis May Fire’s 15+ show on Friday, March 14 at Mill City Nights originally appeared on Tanner’s website. Tanner will be regularly shooting shows at THE GARAGE as well as other all-ages and 15+ shows in the Twin Cities.]

by Tanner

I love going to Mill City Nights to see shows. This was no exception to the awesomeness that usually goes down there. Beartooth got the show rolling as the opener. The guys in Hands Like Houses took the stage next. A Skylit Drive came up and got crazy. Then, The Word Alive. Dudes were a blast. To finish the crowd off, Memphis May Fire made a hell of an appearance. All in all it was an awesome show. Good vibes.Â Oh, and whoever was running lights deserves a raise.
