LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 3/12: The Wonder Years, Fireworks, Citizen, Real Friends and Modern Baseball

Posted on March 13, 2014 by THE GARAGE in Uncategorized // 0 Comments

review and photos by Kelsey

video by Rico

THE GARAGE had a full house for The Wonder Years show this past Wednesday. Modern Baseball, Real Friends, Citizen, and Fireworks played prior to The Wonder Years. The entire crowd was shoulder-to-shoulder for the entire show. Sometimes at shows the crowd doesn’t know the opening bands and will wait to arrive at the show. This was not the case. All of the bands had fans in the crowd screaming all of the words to their songs. You can see in some of the photos how energetic the crowd was. People were jumping around and moshing themselves ridiculous.
