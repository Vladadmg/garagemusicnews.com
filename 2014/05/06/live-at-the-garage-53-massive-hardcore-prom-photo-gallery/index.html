LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 5/3: Massive Hardcore Prom photo gallery

Posted on May 6, 2014 by THE GARAGE in Uncategorized // 0 Comments

photos #1 by Genna

photos #2 by Tanner

On Saturday, May 3, From Concept to Chronicle hosted Hardcore Prom, headlined by Roads of Glass, featuring From Concept to Chronicle, Upon His Flesh, At Mourning’s End, Wanderer, Ataraxia, Beyond Eternity, and Foundations with DJs Noceurz, J Alexander, and Ganzza.

Go get something to drink and some snacks before you settle in for these two huge galleries.
