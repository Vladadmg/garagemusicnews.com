LIVE AT THE GARAGE | Video 1/11: Sims and Astronautalis

Posted on January 28, 2014 by The Garage in Uncategorized // 0 Comments

Video by Rico

“Last Saturday THE GARAGE hosted a benefit by nonprofits To the Bridge Foundation and the Minnesota Music Coalition to raise awareness about substance abuse with a bill of top local bands, the headline being a collab set between hip-hop powerhouses Astronautalis and Sims. On an emotionally heavy night supporting an important cause, the musicians kept the audience focused and entertained, sharing stories and pledging support between top notch performances.” Read more…

Below are live videos of Sims and Astronautalis from their collab set. For more pictures, video, and a review check out our first post about the show here.
