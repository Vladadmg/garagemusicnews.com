LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 7/17: Mod Sun sells out…twice!

Posted on July 18, 2014 by THE GARAGE in Uncategorized // 0 Comments

photos by Nikki and Riley

video by Rico

Thursday, July 17, Mod Sun played a $1 secret show at THE GARAGE, having announced it only the previous evening. With only 24 hours of notice, enough fans showed up–lined up down and past our building–to sell out THE GARAGE twice! So what did Mod Sun do? PLAY TWO SETS. By the end of the night 1,091 people got tickets and Mod Sun stayed around after for a meet and greet, signing autographs and taking selfies with fans.

What. A. Night.

All the evidence, including a video recap, is below.
