LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 8/26: Pride Fest

Posted on August 29, 2016 by logancatalystmusic in Show Photos // 0 Comments

 

by Ryan Johnson and Bethany Schreiner

Photos from Friday’s show at THE GARAGE: Pride Fest! On four stages the show featured Soulkeeper, Stillbreather, Turn the Screw, Tied to Tides, Inconsistent, More Weight, Roads of Glass, Rally Cap, Gramma, Wolf Skin, Unturned, Familiar Theme, Rig Time!, The Home Stretch, Focus Ring, Q-Pup, and Holdridge Park!
