LIVE IN MPLS | 9/28 Car Seat Headrest & Lucy Dacus at The Cedar Cultural Center

rjohnsonphoto
2 years ago

Car Seat Headrest returned to the Twin Cities last night with opening act Lucy Dacus. With the chart hitting success of their album Teens of Denial, the buzz about the band has been growing since the LP’s release this past May. The quality tunes of Teens of Denial, paired with the excitement around the album after being featured as “Album of the Week” by The Current this last week, lead to this sold out show to be one for the books.
