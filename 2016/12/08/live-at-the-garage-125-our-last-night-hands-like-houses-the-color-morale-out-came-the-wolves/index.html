LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 12/5: Our Last Night, Hands Like Houses, The Color Morale, Out Came The Wolves!

Posted on December 8, 2016 by Hana S. Photography in Show Photos // 0 Comments

By Hana Simons and Bethany Schreiner

Our Last Night celebrated their 5th sold out show with us on Monday night. The crowd picked up their energy and excitement as Our Last Night Covered “Cold Water” with Trenton Woodley of Hands like Houses (Garrett Rapp of The Color Morale had missed his cue to come on stage for the song). Regardless, they had laughed it off and made jokes. This show was truly something special. From Garrett saying that the Garage is his favorite venue to play to Trevor Wentworth gushy about how much he loves their fans and vowing they’ll be back soon. Such an incredible tour with wonderful bands.

Bethany
