(Some of) The Things Prince Left Behind

Posted on April 25, 2016 by THE GARAGE in news, Uncategorized // 0 Comments

by Genna Skouge

Prince was an incredibly talented musician that left us too early. Upon hearing the news of the Minnesota music legend’s death on April 21, 2016 I had a lot of emotions. First it was utter shock and then it turned into sadness and then it felt like a piece of my heart had just broken off and I cried. On April 24, 2016 I bought some flowers and headed over to Paisley Park to pay my respects. As I pulled in to the park I saw thousands of people and a lot of purple, the color of peace. What a color to be remembered by.

 

Prince will remain loved and praised for years and years to come. Because of the impact he has made on people all around the world with his music, his love for people and his acts of kindness, his legend and his music will live on forever.
