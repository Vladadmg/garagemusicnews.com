LIVE IN MN | WARPED TOUR 2017

Posted on July 25, 2017 by Hana S. Photography in Show Photos // 0 Comments

by Hana Simons

Vans Warped Tour kicked off their seventh week of tour with local band Villain of the Story on main stage. After seven weeks of blasting heat or pouring rain, Minnesota offered a beautifully breezy day. Making it one of the best dates of Warped so far as many bands said as they were preforming.

Here are some photos from this wonderful day.

Bands featured (in order): Bad Omens, Villain of the Story, Jule Vera, Dance Gavin Dance, Counterparts, Sworn In, Watsky, Our Last Night, Knocked Loose, Sylar, American Authors, Andy Black, Courage My Love, Hands Like Houses, HUNDREDTH, Being As An Ocean, Neck Deep, and Memphis May Fire!!!
