LIVE IN MN | All-ages Hip Hop Show Comes to Duluth

Posted on February 22, 2017 by maddysiiter in Announcement, news // 0 Comments

By: Maddy Siiter  

Duluth Homegrown Music Festival presents its first ever all-ages hip hop showcase on Friday, February 24 at the Red Herring Lounge in Duluth.

The lineup features eight acts from Minneapolis, Red Lake and Duluth, including Dwynell Roland, Baby Shel and EddieDition.

Doors open at 7pm and the show will begin at 8pm. 5$ cover charge at the door.

The Duluth music scene is not known for having many youth-accessible shows, so this event is definitely something to check out and help the all-ages scene grow.

Homegrown Music Festival is a 19-year-old festival in Duluth showcasing local music. What started as a birthday party with a couple of bands has turned into an 8-day music festival with around 200 bands playing in venues all over the Twin Ports. Homegrown 2017 is April 30 – May 7.

What: Homegrown Music Festival Presents: All-Ages Hip Hop

https://www.facebook.com/events/1815586298705864/

Where: The Red Herring Lounge in Duluth

208 E 1st St., Duluth MN, 55802

When: Friday, February 24 at 7pm.

Cost: $5 at door
