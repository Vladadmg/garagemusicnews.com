LIVE AT THE GARAGE | 12/6: Adestria, Kingdom of Giants, Dayseeker, Death of an Era, more

Posted on December 10, 2013 by The Garage in Uncategorized // 0 Comments

(Click for .gif)

Photos by Meghan and Austin

Video by Rico

On Friday, THE GARAGE was invaded by the tour package of Adestria, Kingdom of Giants, Dayseeker, and Death of an Era, supported by The Broken Are Crowned, Neuralizer, Descripti()ns, Anchors Below the Abyss, and At Mourning’s End. It was a great show, with maximum metalcore. Our photographer Meghan was impressed by the extent to which the entire bill interacted with the crowd, supported each other, and encouraged pits. Keep it up and rock on!
